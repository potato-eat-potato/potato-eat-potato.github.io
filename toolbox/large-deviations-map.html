The Logic of the Area

  1. Rates exist: \(\mathbb{P}(S_n/n \approx a) \approx e^{-n\Lambda^*(a)}\) (Cramer theorem).
  2. Rates are dual: $^* = $ Legendre transform of the log-MGF; tilting parameter ↔︎ target value.
  3. Rates are entropies: the cost of a tilt is its relative entropy; optimal tilts make rare events typical (Change of measure).
  4. Everything else is transport: contraction principles, Varadhan — statements known, proofs not yet processed; don’t fake it.

Where LD Plugs Into My Program

  • Gaussian tails: \(\Lambda^*(a) = a^2/(2\sigma^2)\) — every DGFF estimate’s inner loop.
  • Field shifts: Cameron–Martin cost \(\frac12\mathcal{E}(h)\) — large deviations of the DGFF itself, with Dirichlet energy as rate function. (This is the deep reason energy appears in both halves of my toolbox.)
