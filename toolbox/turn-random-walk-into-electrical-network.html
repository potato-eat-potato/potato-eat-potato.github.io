Tool: Turn random walk into electrical network

One-Sentence Summary

Place a unit resistor on every edge; the walk’s hitting probabilities, visit counts, and recurrence become voltages, currents, and resistances — and physical reasoning becomes rigorous.

What Problem Does This Solve?

Quantitative questions about reversible walks on graphs whose geometry is too irregular for exact computation but amenable to physical estimates.

The Dictionary

Probability Electricity
\(\mathbb{P}_x(\tau_a < \tau_z)\) voltage \(v(x)\) (normalized \(v(a)=1\), \(v(z)=0\))
escape probability \(\mathbb{P}_a(\tau_z < \tau_a^+)\) \(\big(c(a)\,R_{\mathrm{eff}}(a,z)\big)^{-1}\)
expected net edge crossings current \(i(e)\) (Unit current flow)
expected visits \(G_D(x,x)\) \(c(x)\,R_{\mathrm{eff}}(x, D^c)\)
recurrence \(R_{\mathrm{eff}}(0, \infty) = \infty\)
commute time \(\mathbb{E}_a\tau_z + \mathbb{E}_z\tau_a\) \(2|E|\,R_{\mathrm{eff}}(a,z)\)

What It Converts

  • from: estimates about random paths
  • to: estimates about one deterministic quadratic form (Dirichlet energy)

Core Idea

Both theories solve the same Dirichlet problem (Turn hitting probability into harmonic function gives the probability side; Kirchhoff + Ohm give the electrical side; the Maximum principle gives uniqueness, so they agree). The payoff is that the electrical side has operations — series, parallel, shorting, cutting, energy bounds — with no probabilistic counterpart.

When Should I Think of This?

  • The graph is reversible and the question is about hitting, escape, recurrence, or visit counts.
  • I want monotonicity: “making the graph bigger/denser helps the walk” — that’s Rayleigh monotonicity, only visible electrically.

Typical Use Cases

  • Pólya recurrence/transience in all dimensions in two pages (Doyle–Snell).
  • Escape probability of SRW on Z2 — shorting annuli.
  • Commute-time bounds for algorithm analysis (where my CS side occasionally meets this).

Where This Appears in My Path

The central object of the Zeitouni reading project, and the title concept of both my talks. If I had to name the single conversion that made me a probabilist, it’s this one.

Current Understanding

Solid for finite networks and unit conductances; comfortable with the infinite-network limit for recurrence criteria.

Open Confusions

  • Non-reversible chains: the dictionary breaks; I don’t know what survives.
