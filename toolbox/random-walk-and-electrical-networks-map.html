The Logic of the Area (four steps)

  1. Hitting probabilities are harmonic → probability questions become Dirichlet problems. Turn hitting probability into harmonic function
  2. Voltages solve the same Dirichlet problems → uniqueness (Maximum principle) forces probability = electricity. Turn random walk into electrical network
  3. Electricity is variational → Dirichlet energy is minimized by truth; competitors give bounds. Thomson principle + Dirichlet principle = the vise; Rayleigh monotonicity = surgery.
  4. One number rules: Effective resistance encodes escape, recurrence, visits, commute times. Use effective resistance to control random walk
