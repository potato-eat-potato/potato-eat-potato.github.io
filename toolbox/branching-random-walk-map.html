The model organism. Object note: Branching random walk. Everything here is training for the DGFF Map — and beautiful in its own right.

The Logic of the Area

  1. One identity to compute: many-to-one turns particle-count first moments into single-walk Cramér estimates.
  2. Speed = entropy/energy crossing: \(\Lambda^*(v^*) = \log m\) (Use Cramer transform for large deviation speed, Entropy versus energy).
  3. Corrections = tree structure: \(-\frac{3}{2\theta^*}\log n\), proved by barrier-truncated second moments — where the genuinely branching-specific technology lives.
  4. Limit law = derivative martingale (Aïdékon) — the deep layer.

The full layered status is in Maximum of branching random walk — that problem note is the real center of this map.
